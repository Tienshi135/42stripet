# User Documentation

## Accessing the Website

Open your web browser and navigate to:
https://stripet.42.fr

You may see a security warning about the self-signed SSL certificate. This is normal for development environments. Click "Advanced" and proceed to the site.

## WordPress Admin Panel

### Logging In

1. Go to `https://stripet.42.fr/wp-admin`
2. Enter your username and password (created during installation)
3. Click "Log In"

You'll be taken to the WordPress Dashboard, the central hub for managing your site.

### Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard shows:
- **At a Glance**: Quick stats (posts, pages, comments)
- **Activity**: Recent posts and comments
- **Quick Draft**: Write a post quickly

## Creating and Managing Content

### Writing a Blog Post

1. From the left sidebar, click **Posts** → **Add New**
2. Enter a title in the "Add title" field
3. Write your content in the text editor
4. Use the toolbar buttons to format text (bold, italic, links, etc.)
5. Add an excerpt (optional summary) in the "Excerpt" box
6. Set a featured image (optional) by clicking "Set featured image"
7. Click **Publish** to make it live, or **Save Draft** to edit later

### Creating a Page

Pages are for static content (About, Contact, CV, etc.).

1. Click **Pages** → **Add New**
2. Follow the same process as creating a post
3. Click **Publish**

Pages don't appear in the blog feed — they're accessed via a menu or direct link.

### Editing Existing Content

1. Click **Posts** or **Pages** in the sidebar
2. Hover over the item you want to edit
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make your changes
5. Click **Update**

### Deleting Content

1. Click **Posts** or **Pages**
2. Hover over the item
3. Click **Trash** (or **Delete Permanently** if already in trash)

## Managing Your Site

### Changing Site Title and Tagline

1. Click **Settings** → **General**
2. Edit "Site Title" and "Tagline" fields
3. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**

### Changing Site URL

1. Click **Settings** → **General**
2. Update "WordPress Address" and "Site Address" if needed
3. Click **Save Changes**

### Managing Users

1. Click **Users** in the sidebar
2. View all users, edit roles, or add new users
3. Click a username to edit their profile

### Managing Comments

1. Click **Comments** in the sidebar
2. View, approve, reject, or delete comments
3. Click on a comment to reply or edit it

## Customizing Your Site

### Changing the Theme

1. Click **Appearance** → **Themes**
2. Browse available themes
3. Click **Activate** on the theme you want
4. Your site's design will update immediately

### Managing Menus

1. Click **Appearance** → **Menus**
2. Create a new menu or edit existing ones
3. Add pages/posts/links to your menu
4. Click **Save Menu**
5. Assign the menu to a location (Header, Footer, etc.)

### Adding Widgets

Widgets are small blocks of content (recent posts, categories, search, etc.).

1. Click **Appearance** → **Widgets**
2. Drag widgets from the left to your sidebar/footer area
3. Configure each widget's settings
4. Widgets update on your site immediately

## Common Tasks

### Publishing Your First Post

1. Click **Posts** → **Add New**
2. Type a title: "Welcome to My Site"
3. Write some content in the editor
4. Click **Publish**
5. View it on your site by clicking "View post"

### Viewing Your Site

Click the site name at the top left of the Dashboard to visit your site's homepage. Or navigate directly to `https://stripet.42.fr`.

### Logging Out

Click your username in the top right corner, then **Log Out**.

## Troubleshooting

### "I forgot my password"

1. Go to `https://stripet.42.fr/wp-login.php`
2. Click "Lost your password?"
3. Enter your username or email
4. Click "Get New Password"
5. Check your email for a password reset link

### "My changes aren't showing"

1. Make sure you clicked **Publish** or **Update**
2. Hard refresh your browser (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
3. Check if you have the right user permissions

### "I can't upload images"

1. Go to **Settings** → **Media**
2. Check that the media upload path is correct
3. Ensure the uploads folder has write permissions
